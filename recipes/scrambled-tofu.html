Scrambled Tofu

Description

Nutritious and filling, the perfect vegan breakfast!

Ingredients

  • 150g of soft tofu
  • one big tomato
  • one small onion
  • some veggie milk
  • one teaspon of turmeric
  • one teaspoon of himalayan salt
  • half teaspoon of black pepper

Steps

  1. Fry tofu on medium heat for a minute
  2. Add chopped tomato and onion
  3. Mix everything and add spices and milk
  4. Cook it until the milk mostly evaporates
