Sandwich

Description

A simple sandwich

Ingredients

  • a slice of bread
  • margarine
  • tomato
  • 2 vegan deli slices

Steps

  1. Spread margarine on the bread
  2. Place the deli slices on the bread
  3. SHAKE IT
  4. Collect food from the ground and assemble again
  5. Chop the tomato and put the slices on top of the vegan deli
